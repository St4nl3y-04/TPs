Welcome to Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance.

Interesting Facts about Mars:

  • Mars has the largest dust storms in the solar system.
  • It has two small moons named Phobos and Deimos.
  • The surface of Mars is covered with iron oxide, giving it a reddish color.
  • Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.
