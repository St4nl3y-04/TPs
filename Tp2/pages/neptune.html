Welcome to Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in the Solar System. It is known for its striking blue color, which is a result of the absorption of red light by methane in its atmosphere.

Interesting Facts about Neptune:

  • Neptune has the strongest winds in the Solar System, reaching speeds of over 1,200 miles per hour.
  • It has 14 known moons, with Triton being the largest.
  • Neptune was discovered in 1846 and is named after the Roman god of the sea.
