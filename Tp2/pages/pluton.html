Welcome to the Pluton Page

Pluton, also known as Pluto, is a dwarf planet in the Kuiper belt. It was discovered in 1930 and was considered the ninth planet in our solar system until 2006.

Interesting Facts about Pluton:

  • Pluton has five known moons, the largest of which is Charon.
  • It has a highly elliptical orbit, taking about 248 years to complete one revolution around the Sun.
  • Pluton is primarily composed of ice and rock.
