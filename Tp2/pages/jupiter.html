Welcome to the Jupiter Page

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, known for its Great Red Spot and many moons.

Interesting Facts about Jupiter:

  • Jupiter is a gas giant and does not have a solid surface.
  • It has a very strong magnetic field and is home to at least 79 moons.
  • The Great Red Spot is a giant storm that has been raging for hundreds of years.
  • Jupiter is primarily composed of hydrogen and helium.
